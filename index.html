How to Use the KAA Family Tree

  1. Type in your first name and last name.
  2. Click on "Go!" button.
  3. Hover over names to see how they are connected to you!
  4. Click on other names to see their tree!

What do all the colors mean?

Purple = The person whose tree has been built

Light Blue = The people directly related to you.

Dark Blue = The people who co-picked up into your tree.

Red = A person who is already somewhere else on the tree. Hovering over them will show you where their counterpart is!

Orange = The direct lineage of the person whose tree has been built

Yellow = The line that connects the person whose tree has been built to whoever you are hovering over
